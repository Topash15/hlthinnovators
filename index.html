About Us

At Health Innovators Consulting, we are committed to partnering with health care startups through every stage of their growth journey. We specialize in providing professional services that position startups for success in the pre-commercialization phase, including product design optimization and go-to-market planning. Our technical guidance includes evidence-based insights on market access, reimbursement, pricing, valuation, and other essential areas. We believe that startups are founded by inventors with remarkable creativity, and as your trusted health innovation partner, we bring industry-leading business expertise to help you overcome obstacles and unlock your full potential.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower early-stage companies to achieve their greatest version of themselves and make a meaningful impact in the healthcare industry. We provide ongoing support through organization building, coaching, and leadership development services for both individuals and teams, and work closely with our clients to develop executable plans that accelerate their time to market. If you're seeking a partner who can help you bring your health innovation from concept to commercialization, look no further than Health Innovators Consulting. We're here to support you every step of the way.

Our Team

Mark Topash

Mark Topash is a seasoned healthcare executive with over 30 years of experience in the industry. He is passionate about bringing innovative and new technologies to the forefront of patient care and recognized the need for comprehensive and sound advice that allows healthcare startups to properly prepare to take their innovative solutions to market.

In response to this need, he founded Health Innovators Consultants, a new firm designed to accelerate a startup's time to market through his expertise and a network of experienced colleagues.

Throughout his career, Mark has held leadership roles at prominent and successful healthcare startups including Cleerly and iRhythm Technologies, value-based care companies like Hill Physicians Medical Group and Evolent Health, national health insurance companies, United Healthcare and Humana, and regional Blue Cross Blue Shield plans in Wisconsin and Pennsylvania.

As a strategic business consultant, Mark brings a wealth of expertise in business strategy, sales, marketing, and operations to help healthcare startups and investors optimize their products and operating models for the real-world clinical setting. He has a proven track record of launching successful products, building strategic partnerships, standing up go-to-market verticals, and driving growth while scaling the business.

Mark's extensive knowledge of the healthcare reimbursement landscape makes him an invaluable resource for startups looking to navigate the complexities of CMS reimbursement processes, MAC coverage policies, and billing code/payment processes, including NTAP, NT-APC, HCPCS, and CPT. He also has experience navigating the AMA CPT Editorial Panel and RUC processes.

Mark is a published author, having co-authored "Cardiac arrhythmia detection outcomes among patients monitored with the Zio patch system: a systematic literature review", which was published in Current Medical Research and Opinion in May 2019.

Mark earned his Masters in Healthcare Administration with an emphasis in Finance and a Bachelor's of Science Degree in healthcare business from the University of Missouri - Columbia. His unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen make him an invaluable asset to healthcare startups looking to accelerate their growth and transform the industry.
